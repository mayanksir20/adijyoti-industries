1. Information We Collect

At Adijyoti Industries, we collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Details: Name, Phone Number, Email Address when you fill our inquiry forms.
  • Business Info: Type of machinery interest, bank details for subsidy consultancy, and project location.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information to improve website performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

To process your machinery orders and provide expert technical support.

To assist you with Government Subsidies (PMFME, PMEGP) and Bank Loan documentations.

To send important updates regarding new industrial technology and solar solutions.
