Who Can
Apply?

Check your eligibility status

Individuals & Groups

Micro-food entrepreneurs, SHGs, FPOs, and Cooperatives.

Age Requirement

The applicant should be above 18 years of age.

Education

At least 8th standard pass (for individuals).

Required Documents

  • 01 Aadhar & PAN Card
  • 02 Bank Passbook / Cancelled Cheque
  • 03 Land/Building Proof (Lease or Own)
  • 04 Quotation for Machinery
  • 05 Project Report (DPR)

*Note: Additional documents like MSME Udyam, FSSAI, or GST registration may be required depending on the project.
